Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the PPID Unhas app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Universitas Hasanuddin (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal information from users.

We do not:

Third Party Access

Since no data is collected, no information is shared with third parties.

The Application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The Application does not collect any information from anyone.
